About

I'm a Senior Product Designer with 8+ years of experience, currently embedded with the Nearpod / Renaissance Learning team through PSh. I design teacher and student-facing features end-to-end, from discovery and research through to QA and the occasional pull request.

My career started in graphic design, which gave me a strong visual foundation I still rely on every day. That background shapes how I approach design systems, visual hierarchy, and the craft side of product work.

Lately I've been focused on AI-powered features: activity suggestions, lesson generation, and an AI teaching assistant concept still in the works. I also use AI extensively in my own workflow, from prototyping with Claude Code to generating documentation and automating design QA.

I work remotely with US-based cross-functional teams from Buenos Aires. I work in English and Spanish, and speak some Portuguese.

Outside of work I knit constantly, always mid-project and usually something more ambitious than I planned. And I share my couch with Helena, a greyhound who has claimed the entire couch as her own.
